What Is the Workflow Builder?

Workflows streamline and organize your business processes by separating complex logic across a series of modules. With Workflows, you can achieve the same result as including everything in one module, but with a smoother end-user End-users, also known as Express Users, are the individuals accessing an application through Express View. In most cases, end-users are the customers using the product. experience. Workflows also let you see what step of the process your end-user completed.

To learn more about the Workflow Builder, visit our Introduction to Workflow article.
