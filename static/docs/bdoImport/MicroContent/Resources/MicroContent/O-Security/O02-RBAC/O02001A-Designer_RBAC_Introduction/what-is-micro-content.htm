What is Designer Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)?

Designer RBAC (role-based access control), also known as Creator Also known as Unqork Users, or Designer Users; is anyone who is inside the Unqork platform. RBAC, manages Creator access to an Unqork Environment, and the individual Workspaces in the environment.

To learn more about Designer RBAC, visit our Introduction to Designer Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) article.
