What Is the Excel CSV Upload and Mapping Snippet?

The Excel CSV Upload and Mapping snippet lets your end-user upload an Excel or CSV (comma-separated values) file to your application. From there, the snippet maps the Excel data to Unqork key names. This means your Excel data becomes Unqork-friendly, so you can reference it in your application. For example, you can easily build a dashboard that displays the data from your uploaded file.

This snippet is ready-to-use. All you do is add it to your environment and add it to your module. Then, your end-user can upload a CSV file in Express View. You can also configure a dashboard to display data from the uploaded file.
