How Do You Add Listings to Your Environment?

To add the listing to your environment:

1. At the top right of the Unqork Designer Platform, click Marketplace.
2. In the Search the Listings Directory field, enter the listing name.
3. Click the listing tile you want to add.
4. Click Install to Environment.
5. Click Install Template.

To learn more about adding and removing listings, visit our Adding and Removing Marketplace Listings article.
