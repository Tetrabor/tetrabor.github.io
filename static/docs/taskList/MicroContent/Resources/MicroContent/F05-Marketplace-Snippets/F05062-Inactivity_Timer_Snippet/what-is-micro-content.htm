What Is the Inactivity Timer Snippet?

The inactivity timer snippet requires an end-user to refresh a page after a period of inactivity. This ensures an end-user doesn't sit idle on a page, taking up valuable resources. You can customize this snippet's action in many ways. You can force the end-user to refresh the page or even lock them out of the application.
