What Are the Charts & Graphs Components?

The Charts & Graphs group of components display aggregated statistics of data. These data visualization tools help end-users End-users, also known as Express Users, are the individuals accessing an application through Express View. In most cases, end-users are the customers using the product. to gather insights from a data set retrieved by a Plug-In component.

To learn more about the Charts & Graphs components, visit our Introduction to Charts & Graphs article.
